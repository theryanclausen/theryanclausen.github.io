Being Human

An anxious young man with relationship problems goes to his family's cabin to clear his head. When he arrives, he finds his girlfriend and bestfriend are already there.

This film was shot at a cabin on the Puget Sound. This film premiered at the Dam Short Film Festival in Boulder City, Nevada and went on to screen at festivals across the country.
