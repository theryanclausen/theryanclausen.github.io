About

Ryan studied filmmaking at the Los Angeles campus of the New York Film Academy. After graduation, Ryan spent a few months working on film sets in and around Los Angeles before returning home to the Pacific Northwest to focus on making his own films. Ryan currently resides in Tucson, Arizona.

In addition to writing and directing his projects, Ryan also produces, edits, and occasionally composes and mixes the soundtrack.

You can keep up with Ryan and his ongoing projects at the following links. If you would like to collaborate with Ryan, please get in touch at theryanclausen@gmail.com.
